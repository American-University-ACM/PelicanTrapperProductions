What We Are About

Pelican Trapper Productions is a collaboration of people, a collaboration of artists. Mainly from the Richmond, VA area. These artists are here to work together, as one for the betterment of their own art and the art of others. This is a place for creativity and sharing. This is a place for people to come together to work to achieve a better understanding of their art. This is a place that won't have hatred toward anyone else in any art form. This.. is a place for you. Simply put, we are here not to beat out someone else, but to work together with that someone else in hopes that we both make it. Everyone is welcome and any ideas are always appreciated.
